About Us

Who we are

A disability support provider that puts people first — always curious, always collaborative, always person-centred.

Our Philosophy

We are curious to get to know you

At Spring 2 Health, we want to get to know all about you as the expert in your own life. We take a person-centred approach to understanding who you are at a deeper level so that we can provide individualised disability support that speaks to exactly who you are.

We operate from a curiosity standpoint and are constantly seeking to learn more about our participants and their unique situations. Our approach is strength-based — we focus on abilities and potential rather than limitations.

We recognise that our participants are experts in their own lives, and we work to empower them to make their own decisions and live their lives to the fullest. We operate from a place of diversity and inclusion, ensuring that everyone feels valued and respected.

Uniquely Tailored to YouEvery participant receives support designed around their individual needs, goals, and aspirations.

Why Spring 2 Health

We provide support differently

Through providing a tailored service unique to you — your obstacles and your aspirations — we deliver care that supports you as an individual.

Adventure Therapy
Innovative outdoor programs using nature and adventure to build resilience, self-esteem, and mental well-being in a supported environment.
Social Work & Advocacy
Our qualified social workers are specialists in navigating complex disability systems, advocating for your rights every step of the way.
Strength-Based Approach
We focus on your abilities, strengths, and potential — not your limitations. Every plan is built around what you can do and where you want to go.
Evidence-Based Therapy
Therapeutic services grounded in current best practice — helping you develop coping strategies and improve mental health and well-being.
Community Connected
We break down barriers to community participation, helping you build meaningful relationships and engage with the things that matter to you.
Trauma-Informed Practice
All our services are delivered through a trauma-informed lens — safe, empowering, and grounded in compassion and respect.
